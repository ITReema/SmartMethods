# Chatbot
Build simple chatbot with App Inventor.

## Requirement
* [MIT App Inventor](https://appinventor.mit.edu)
                          
## Chatbot Questions
* Hello/Hi
* How are you?
* Who built you/Who made you?
* What is your name?
* Goodbye/bye
* Thank you/Thanks
* Good morning/evening/afternoon/night
* How many language can you speak?
* Who are you?
* Are you real?
* Are you still there?
